ARE YOU HAVING AN EMERGENCY?

Emergency Dentist

Do not hesitate to seek emergency dental care if you experience any of the following symptoms:

  • A Severe Toothache or Bleeding
  • Broken Tooth or Jaw
  • Lost Dental Filling or Crown
  • Object Stuck in Teeth

We offer Same Day Treatment! At South Florida Dental Center, we do not want our patients to wait in pain. We offer SAME DAY treatment for all dental emergencies.
